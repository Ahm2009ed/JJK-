Culling games

An epic fight

The Culling Game takes place across multiple colonies where sorcerers fight to gain points and add rules to eventually save Tsumiki. Yuji and Megumi enter the Tokyo No. 1 Colony but get separated; Yuji meets Higuruma and convinces him to help after a trial-like battle, while Megumi defeats Reggie and gains points. In the Sendai Colony, Yuta faces several powerful opponents in a deadlocked battle, defeats them all, and collects their points without killing them. In Tokyo No. 2 Colony, Hakari fights Kashimo after saving Panda; their intense battle ends with Hakari winning and recruiting Kashimo as an ally. Meanwhile, in the Lake Gosho Colony, Kenjaku hunts players and fights Takaba, but is ultimately killed by Yuta. However, before dying, Kenjaku transfers control of ending the Culling Game to Sukuna, making the overall situation more dangerous.
